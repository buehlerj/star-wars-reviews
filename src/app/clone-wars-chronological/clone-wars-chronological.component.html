Star Wars: The Clone Wars Chronological Order

Star Wars: The Clone Wars is a show that initially released as a movie on August 15th, 2008, then turned into a TV show that ran intermittently from October 3, 2008 to May 4th, 2020. During this time, many different stories were told about the events of the Clone Wars, bundled together in what arcs. These arcs are told to the viewer in story form, as if a recollection of the Clone Wars years. The Clone Wars took place between the years of 22-19 BBY (Before the Battle of Yavin). That said, these episodes did not get released in Chronological order.

To the left is a list of all Clone Wars episodes (including the Movie) sorted by chronological order. Each episode is color coded to get a better understanding of how the show was initially envisioned by George Lucas and Dave Filoni.

Although release order and chronological order differ significantly, there are many continuous stories told in release order. I have made a separation line between jumps in episodes to help keep track of where the clone wars episodes change in historical time.

How To Watch Chronologically

Each episode has a Disney+ link to the right of it. If you have a Disney+ account, you can click that link to jump directly to that episode.

1. Start at the top! Click on the link for the first episode at the top.

2. Continue down the list. If you run into one of the dividers, close the Disney+ video you're watching and click on the new link. If the next episode on the list is in the same container, then you can just let the Disney+ auto-play continue (or click on the "Play Next Episode In" button).

Example: If you just finished the episode "Cat and Mouse", close out of Disney+ and click on the link for Hidden Enemy.

Example 2: If you just finished the episode "Ambush", continue watching through Disney+ until you've reached the episode "Trespass"
